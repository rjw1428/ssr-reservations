The Burwell Project is a historic Salem homesite that has been renovated and restored over the years and turned into an ideal small business office collaborative. The charm of this building, originally constructed in 1870, has been preserved through the thoughtful stewardship of revered Salem architect T.A. Carter Jr, who occupied the building for several decades and even through his retirement. A cosmetic facelift by the current owner brings a comfortable modern feeling to the space, while honoring the dignified grace of it's heritage.

The downtown location is ideal for many aspects - walking distance to the Roanoke County and City of Salem municipal buildings and courthouses, and countless restaurants and shops for our renters and their clientele. It's conveniently accessed from exit 140 off Interstate 81. The collaborative spirit of the office space and layout lends itself to networking and cross-servicing. Your quaint "one-man show" is no longer lonely, but rather enhanced with other professionals offering high level services to their clients as well.
